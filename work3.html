UCTV's Comedy Jam (2019)

The Project

This was one of my earlier projects as a graphic designer at UCTV. I was tasked by the director of the Entertainment department to create an eye-catching poster for his comedy show event. I created a neon sign look on a brick background to evoke that classic comedy club look, while also going for a realistic aesthetic. I created this in Photoshop.
