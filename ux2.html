The Pound (2019)

The Project

The Pound is an on-campus organization I am part of that seeks to highlight student-athletes both on and off the field. I was tasked this semester with the creation of a website for The Pound which will serve as a hub for all of our social media platforms. It will also be a place where our members, as well as student-athletes, can write and share articles.
