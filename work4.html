Self Portrait (2017)

The Project

When I was applying to colleges and submitting my portfolio, I realized several of them required a self-portrait, which I did not have. Initially, I was planning on doing a traditional pencil or charcoal portrait of myself, but realized that it was okay to apply my talents for design in a more artistic setting. Similar to the sirection I took with my Joker piece, I created this using paths and blending in Illustrator, and wanted to go for a more avant-garde representation of myself. I opted to include piano keys dripping from my head instead of just leaving my face blank to add some uniqueness to the portrait. The piano keys represent my love for music and how something is always playing in my mind. This musical influence seeps out into my personality and, really, my entire life and this portrait captures that.
