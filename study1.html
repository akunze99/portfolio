Innovate Wellness Challenge

Challenge

My task was to rename and re brand the Wellness Case Competition, which was a recent development at UConn. The competition gave students the opportunity to form teams and fund a project that is aimed at engaging the student body in health and wellness. This then developed into a permanent division of Student Affairs, known as Innovate Wellness, who sponsor the now renamed Innovate Wellness Challenge. The absence of a consistent brand gave me much creative freedom to accurately present the excitement and opportunity of the program.

Outcome

The name I suggested, Innovate Wellness Challenge, replaced the former Wellness Case Competition giving it a more approachable and understandable title. Then, I designed their new logo, with a brand color scheme and design sensibility. That design scheme has since successfully been applied to all branded public material displayed across the university, and internal material.
